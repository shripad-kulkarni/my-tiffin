About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

When peoples are away from their homes for work, study, etc. in a new city, they are looking for good food. And they are looking for the food which is afforded to them that’s why they are going to the mess. Sometimes there is not a good menu in one of the messes. That’s why they are looking at the menu in another mess physically.

That’s why I have provided a menu through an online mode or digital mode. And have provided connections between users and mess. I have provided the actual location of that mess where the menu is good and users want to go. Users are also allowed to give feedback on the respective mess they are visited.

That’s why the objective of the system is that users can see the good menu among the various mess menu without going physically using this website. That can save the time and money of users.
